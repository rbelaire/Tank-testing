Why Testing Matters

Federal Mandate

Underground storage tank systems are regulated under EPA 40 CFR Part 280. The federal government requires regular testing and inspection to prevent groundwater contamination and ensure public safety.

Louisiana Requirements

Louisiana Department of Environmental Quality (LDEQ) enforces stricter state-level standards. Non-compliance can result in penalties, operational restrictions, and enforcement actions.

Testing Schedule

Requirements vary: some systems require annual testing, others every three years, and monthly inspections are standard. We track your schedule and keep you compliant.

Non-Compliance Consequences

Failing to maintain compliance can result in significant civil penalties, temporary or permanent delivery prohibition, mandatory system upgrades, and groundwater remediation liability. [CUSTOM COMPLIANCE NOTE]
